# Research Projects

<div class="project">
  <h3 class="project-title">Robust Full-Body 3D Human Pose Estimation</h3>
  <p class="project-meta">Master's Research | African Institute of Mathematical Sciences | Sept 2024 - July 2025</p>
  <p class="project-description">
    Developing a composition of experts framework that combines SMPLest-X, WiLoR, and EMOCA for real-time full-body reconstruction. The system performs parameter-level fusion to achieve superior hand and face details compared to existing baselines like FrankMocap. This research addresses challenges in robust 3D human pose estimation from monocular images.
  </p>
  <p><strong>Technologies:</strong> Python, PyTorch, SMPL-X model</p>

  <p class="project-meta">Engineering Capstone Project | Benin | July - Dec 2023</p>
